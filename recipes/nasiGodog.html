Odin Recepis

Nasi Godog Recepis

  • Ingredients

    1. The Main Components

      • 1 plate White rice (use "old" or slightly dry rice so it doesn't get too mushy).
      • 100g Shredded chicken (boiled or fried beforehand).
      • 2 Eggs (lightly beaten).
      • 30g Yellow noodles or rice vermicelli (optional, for extra texture).
      • Vegetables: Cabbage and Choy Sum (roughly sliced), 1 Tomato (cut into wedges).
      • 500ml Chicken broth (or water with chicken bouillon).

    2. The Spice Paste (Blend these)

      • 3 cloves Garlic.
      • 2 Candlenuts (toasted or fried for a nuttier flavor).
      • 1/2 tsp Whole white peppercorns.
      • Salt & Chicken powder to taste.

    3. Garnishes & Sides

      • Fried shallots.
      • Celery/Cilantro (finely chopped).
      • Bird's eye chilies (keep them whole for a kick of heat).
      • Crackers (Kerupuk).

  • Instructions

    1. Sautéing the Spices

      1. Heat a little oil in a wok or deep pan.
      2. Sauté the Spice Paste until it is fragrant and turns slightly golden. Make sure it’s well-cooked so the raw taste of the candlenuts disappears.
      3. Add the tomato wedges and stir until they soften.

    2. Preparing the Broth & Eggs

      1. Pour the Chicken Broth into the pan and bring it to a boil.
      2. Add the shredded chicken.
      3. Slowly pour in the beaten eggs while stirring gently in a circular motion. This creates beautiful "egg ribbons" or a silky texture in the broth.

    3. Cooking the Rice & Veggies

      1. Add the white rice (and noodles/vermicelli if using). Stir well to combine.
      2. Toss in the cabbage and Choy Sum.
      3. Season with salt, pepper, and chicken powder. Give it a quick taste and adjust as needed.
      4. Simmer for just a minute until the vegetables wilt and the rice absorbs some of the broth. It should be "soupy" (nyemek), not dry.

    4. The Final Touch

      1. Pour the rice and broth into a deep plate or a large bowl.
      2. Top with a generous sprinkle of fried shallots and chopped celery.
      3. Serve immediately while piping hot with crackers on the side.
